About Font Folder

Font Folder is a modern and user-friendly online tool that allows you to explore and preview text in over 100 unique fonts. Whether you’re a designer, blogger, or someone who loves experimenting with typography, Font Folder helps you visualize how your name, title, or article looks in different styles. With an intuitive interface, color and bold options, and instant font preview on demand, it’s the perfect place to find the font that fits your creative vision.

How it works: Type your text in the editor above, use the controls to adjust size, color, and boldness, then click any font card below to apply that font. (Note: font previews are not auto-converted across all fonts when you type — this behavior is intentionally disabled to keep previews consistent and avoid performance issues.)
