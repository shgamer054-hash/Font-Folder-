About Font Folder

Font Folder is a colorful and user-friendly online tool that lets you explore and preview text in a variety of fonts. Type your article in the input box above and click any font card to see your text in that style. This demo disables automatic conversion of your text to all fonts to keep the experience simple and performance-friendly.
